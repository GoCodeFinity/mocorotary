Rotary Club of Monroe County

Welcome!

We are a service organization for of Monroe County. ALL service-minded people are welcome. We are currently using ZOOM meetings during COVID-19 lockdowns, on Wednesdays at 6:30 p.m. If we are not in lockdown, PLAN to attend our socials at 6:30 p.m. on Wednesday at various Monroe County locations (check the Club Events). Our regular meeting follows, from 7:00-8:00 p.m., with guest speakers from around the metropolitan area. Topics range from member information and local help to speakers on crime investigations. For more information on our current service projects, check out the Community Service page.

To learn more about Rotary, please watch this short video "There's no limit to what we can do."
