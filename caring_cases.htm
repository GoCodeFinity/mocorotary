Caring Cases

Our club is proud to present Caring Cases, a new program that will benefit youth in Monroe County who are suffering personal trauma, such as entering foster care, domestic violence, or loss of their belongings in a disaster.

Caring Cases are filled with toiletries and personal items like pajamas, small toys, and books, and are meant to comfort and help ease a child’s mind. Having something to call their own (over which they have sole control) can be important in times of crisis.

Do you know a child who needs a Caring Case? If so, please download our application here (link), complete, and send to RotaryCaringCases@gmail.com
